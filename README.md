# 🧬 BioRender Infographic Protocol  
**Assignment 2 – Graphical Abstract (Porcine Muscle Function Study)**

---

## 🎯 Objective
To design a **horizontal graphical abstract** that visually represents a standardized 6-step protocol for in vivo skeletal muscle function assessment in porcine DMD/BMD models, ensuring clarity, scientific accuracy, and structured workflow representation.

---

## 🧪 Step 1: Selection of Research Content

A peer-reviewed research paper focusing on **in vivo skeletal muscle function assessment in porcine models of muscular dystrophy (DMD/BMD)** was selected.

The study outlines a **6-step experimental protocol** involving:
- Animal preparation  
- Instrument calibration  
- Physiological monitoring  
- Electrical stimulation  
- Force measurement  
- Data analysis  

These steps were extracted and converted into a visual workflow.

---

## 🖥️ Step 2: BioRender Setup

- Platform: BioRender  
- Canvas size: **1600 × 900 px (horizontal layout)**  
- Background: White  

A horizontal layout was chosen to represent a **left-to-right sequential workflow**.

---

## 🎨 Step 3: Structural Layout Design

The infographic was organized into **six stepwise panels arranged horizontally**:

1. Pre-experimental acclimatization (~2 weeks)  
2. System setup & calibration (~30 min)  
3. General anesthesia & monitoring (~30 min)  
4. Positioning & electrode placement (~10 min)  
5. Force measurement (~40 min)  
6. Data analysis (~1 day)  

Below the main workflow, additional sections were included:
- Key readouts  
- Protocol overview  
- Equipment highlights  
- Notes  
- Animal models  

---

## 🧩 Step 4: Icon Selection and Placement

Relevant BioRender icons were used to represent experimental elements:

- Pig model illustrations  
- Monitoring systems and anesthesia equipment  
- Electrode placement visuals  
- Graphical outputs (force curves, bar charts)  
- Data analysis systems  

Icons were placed consistently within each step panel to maintain alignment and visual balance.

---

## 🔄 Step 5: Workflow Representation

- Directional arrows were used to connect all six steps  
- A **linear progression (left → right)** was maintained  
- Supporting panels summarize overall experimental flow  

This improves interpretability and logical sequencing.

---

## 🎨 Step 6: Color Coding Strategy

A limited and consistent color palette was used:

- 🟢 Green → Acclimatization  
- 🔵 Blue → Setup & measurement  
- 🟣 Purple → Anesthesia  
- 🟠 Orange → Electrode placement  
- 🔴 Red → Data analysis  

Color coding helps distinguish experimental stages clearly.

---

## 🏷️ Step 7: Text and Labeling

- Title placed at top center (≤12 words)  
- Subtitle added below title (1 sentence description)  

Each step panel includes:
- Step number  
- Step title  
- Duration  
- Key procedures  
- Important parameters  

Text was kept concise to maintain readability.
